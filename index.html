Trash Track is an ecosystem Android app that empowers you to track your waste, learn responsible disposal methods, and connect with local recycling initiatives.

Download the App

Features

  • Track your waste composition (recyclables, organics, etc.)
  • Learn proper disposal methods for different waste types
  • Find nearby recycling centers and waste collection points

Meet the Developer

Trash Track is the brainchild of Griffins Mudenyo, a passionate Android developer driven by a desire to create a sustainable future. With a dedication to user-friendly design and eco-conscious solutions, Griffins is committed to making waste management accessible and impactful.
